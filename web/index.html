Is there a complete cure?

Cystic fibrosis currently has no universal complete cure, but outcomes improve strongly with modern treatment, especially CFTR modulator therapies for eligible mutations.

Core treatment pillars

Airway clearance, inhaled therapies, targeted antibiotics, enzyme replacement, high-calorie nutrition, and specialist follow-up.

Prevention focus

Prevent complications and infections: vaccination, hand hygiene, early treatment, adherence, and routine lung monitoring.

Family planning & genetics

Carrier screening and genetic counseling are important for families with known CF history.

What is CF?

CF is an inherited condition affecting salt-water movement in cells, leading to thick mucus in lungs and digestive system.

Common symptoms

Chronic cough, recurrent chest infections, wheezing, poor weight gain, greasy stools, and sinus issues.

How diagnosis is done

Sweat chloride test, genetic testing, newborn screening, lung function tests like FEV1, and clinical evaluation.

When to seek care urgently

Breathing distress, persistent fever, blood in sputum, dehydration, rapid decline in exercise tolerance.

United States

Newborn screening and CF center-based management are widely used. Research focuses on early diagnosis, genotype-driven therapy, and outcomes tracking.

United Kingdom

Registry-driven care models and national guidance have supported longitudinal CF outcome analysis and treatment optimization.

Europe (Multi-country)

Cross-country registries and collaborative studies evaluate survival trends, CFTR modulator impact, and quality-of-care variation.

Australia & Canada

Strong CF registry systems and specialist clinics contribute to real-world evidence on disease progression and intervention benefits.

Prior Research Positioning

This project contributes by combining: synthetic minority data generation, federated non-IID training, edge-ready inference, and clinical-style decision support UI.

Why edge for CF screening?

Low-latency local predictions, reduced dependency on cloud connectivity, and improved privacy posture for sensitive health data.

Current readiness

Checkpoint-based local inference, API health checks, browser UI, and feature-consistent preprocessing from training to deployment.

Next optimization targets

Model quantization, ONNX/TorchScript export, resource-aware scheduling, and secure model update + rollback strategy.

Clinical governance

Maintain explainability notes, threshold calibration, audit logs, and clear “screening support only” safeguards in all deployments.
